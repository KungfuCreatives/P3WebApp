About Pinnacle 3 Learning

Pinnacle 3 Learning has grown from its creation in 2005 into a premier project management training company with training locations in most major cities within the US. We have a dedicated staff whose top priority is our students' success.

Specializing in Project Management training for a wide range of industries and government agencies, our instructors are experts in the subject they teach. Our courses are designed for Project Managers who are seeking PMP certification, and for individuals who are looking to expand their knowledge in certain areas of project management or quality management.

Classes are also available to introduce individuals and organizations to Project Management. Courses can be customized to fit the specific business needs of your organization.
